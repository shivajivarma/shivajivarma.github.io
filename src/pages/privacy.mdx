# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 3, 2026

The **redvars/peacock** project is committed to providing a high-quality, performant, and private web experience. This statement outlines our approach to privacy for this static site.

## 1. No Data Collection
This is a static website. We do not require user registration, and we do not collect, store, or process any personal data from our visitors.

* We do not use databases to store user information.
* We do not track your IP address.
* We do not collect browser fingerprinting data.

## 2. Cookies and Tracking
We believe in a clean browsing experience.

* **Cookies:** This site does not use cookies for tracking or advertising.
* **Third-Party Analytics:** We do not use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) that track users across different websites.

## 3. Third-Party Services
This site is built using the Peacock component library and may be hosted on static hosting providers (such as GitHub Pages, Vercel, or Netlify). These providers may collect standard server logs (such as IP addresses and timestamps) for security purposes and to maintain service stability. We do not have access to this data for the purpose of identifying individuals.

## 4. External Links
Our site may contain links to other websites (e.g., GitHub repositories or documentation). Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personally identifiable information.

## 5. Changes to This Statement
This privacy policy is subject to change without notice. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.
